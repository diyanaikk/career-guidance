    Top MBBS Colleges in India

    AIIMS Delhi

    AIIMS Delhi is renowned for world-class medical education and cutting-edge research. It offers premier faculty, infrastructure, and patient care exposure.

    Eligibility: NEET score, 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology

    Rating: ★ 4.9/5

    Christian Medical College (CMC), Vellore

    CMC Vellore is one of India’s most respected institutions, known for academic excellence and community health programs.

    Eligibility: NEET score, 10+2 with PCB, selection test/interview

    Rating: ★ 4.8/5

    JIPMER Puducherry

    JIPMER is an autonomous institute under the Government of India, offering top-notch education with minimal fees and excellent facilities.

    Eligibility: NEET score, 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology

    Rating: ★ 4.7/5
