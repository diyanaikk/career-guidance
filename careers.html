Explore Various Career Paths

We’ve got something for everyone – no matter your stream or passion!

Career Paths

Doctor / Medicine

Care for people's health, treat illnesses, and work in clinics or hospitals. Requires NEET exam in India.

Lawyer / Legal

Uphold justice and solve disputes. Study law (LLB) after 12th via CLAT or other law entrance exams.

Chartered Accountant (CA)

Manage company finances, audits and taxation. Commerce stream + CA exams through ICAI.

Fashion Designer

Create beautiful clothing and accessories. Design schools like NIFT and Pearl Academy offer programs.

Psychologist

Understand human minds and emotions. Study BA/BSc Psychology and go for higher studies or practice.

IAS / Civil Services

Work in Indian government administration through UPSC exams. Open to all streams.

Chef / Culinary Arts

Love food? Turn it into a career! Hotel management courses and culinary schools across India help you start.

Entrepreneur

Start your own business! Any stream, but passion, strategy, and resilience are key.

Animator / Game Designer

Bring characters and worlds to life through animation or gaming. Courses in design + software skills needed.

Engineer

Design and build solutions in technology, construction, electronics and more. JEE Main/Advanced for top colleges.

Journalist

Report news, write stories and inform the public. Study mass communication or journalism after 12th.

Government Jobs

Banking, railways, SSC, PSU and more. Competitive exams open for many streams with stable career options.
