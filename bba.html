    BBA - Top Colleges & Eligibility

    NMIMS Mumbai

    NMIMS Mumbai offers a world-class BBA program with a focus on leadership, innovation, and entrepreneurial skills.

    Eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 50%, NMIMS NPAT Entrance Exam

    Rating: ★ 4.8/5

    Christ University

    Christ University provides a comprehensive BBA program, offering excellent exposure to business practices and international standards.

    Eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 60%, Christ University Entrance Exam

    Rating: ★ 4.7/5

    IIM Indore (IPM)

    IIM Indore’s Integrated Program in Management (IPM) is a unique 5-year program that combines undergraduate and post-graduate studies.

    Eligibility: 10+2, IPM Aptitude Test

    Rating: ★ 5/5
