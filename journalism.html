    BA Journalism - Top Colleges & Eligibility

    IIMC (Indian Institute of Mass Communication)

    IIMC is one of India's premier institutions for journalism and mass communication education, offering programs in various journalism streams.

    Eligibility: Graduation in any discipline, entrance exam (IIMC Entrance)

    Rating: ★ 4.9/5

    XIC Mumbai (Xavier Institute of Communications)

    XIC Mumbai is another leading institution for journalism education, known for its well-rounded curriculum and training in media and communications.

    Eligibility: Graduation in any discipline, entrance exam (XIC Entrance)

    Rating: ★ 4.7/5

    Delhi University (DU)

    Delhi University offers one of the most reputed journalism courses in India, with a vast network and excellent placements.

    Eligibility: 10+2 with English, entrance exam (DU JAT)

    Rating: ★ 4.8/5
