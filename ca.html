    CA Foundation - Top Colleges & Eligibility

    Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI)

    ICAI is the governing body for Chartered Accountancy in India. The CA Foundation course is a 4-month preparatory course.

    Eligibility: 10+2, No entrance exam required, but registration with ICAI is required.

    Rating: ★ 4.9/5

    AKPCA (A. K. P. Chartered Accountants)

    A.K.P. Chartered Accountants offers coaching for CA Foundation with high success rates in India. They focus on theoretical and practical knowledge.

    Eligibility: 10+2, Admission through direct enrollment.

    Rating: ★ 4.5/5
