    B.Des (Design) - Top Colleges & Eligibility

    NID Ahmedabad (National Institute of Design)

    NID Ahmedabad is one of India's top institutions for design education, offering a diverse range of design courses and a globally recognized curriculum.

    Eligibility: 10+2, NID Entrance Exam (Design Aptitude Test)

    Rating: ★ 5/5

    NIFT Delhi (National Institute of Fashion Technology)

    NIFT Delhi is a premier institution for fashion and design studies, offering courses in fashion design, textile design, and more.

    Eligibility: 10+2, NIFT Entrance Exam

    Rating: ★ 4.8/5

    Pearl Academy

    Pearl Academy is known for its high-quality design programs and offers courses in fashion, communication, and product design.

    Eligibility: 10+2, Pearl Academy Entrance Exam

    Rating: ★ 4.7/5
